At ShaadiPariwar, the safety and well-being of children is our highest priority. This Child Safety Policy outlines our strict measures to prevent child exploitation, abuse, and unauthorized access to our platform. 1. Age Restriction (18+ Only Platform) Our platform is strictly intended for use by individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not permit minors to create accounts, access, or use our services under any circumstances. We take reasonable steps to prevent underage access, including account verification measures where appropriate. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will immediately suspend or terminate the account. 2. Commitment to Child Safety Although our platform is designed exclusively for adults, we are fully committed to preventing any misuse that could harm minors. We enforce strict safeguards to ensure that no child-related exploitation or unsafe behavior occurs on our platform. 3. Zero Tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy against any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation. This includes: Uploading, sharing, or storing any child sexual abuse material (CSAM) Attempting to engage with minors in any inappropriate or illegal manner Any content that depicts, promotes, or sexualizes individuals under 18 Grooming behavior, coercion, or exploitation involving minors Any violation will result in immediate removal of content, permanent account termination, and reporting to appropriate law enforcement authorities. 4. Monitoring and Enforcement We implement proactive and reactive measures to detect and prevent harmful activity, including: Content review systems User reporting tools Account monitoring for suspicious behavior When violations are identified, we take immediate action, including removal of content and escalation to relevant authorities such as child protection organizations. 5. Reporting Mechanism We provide users with the ability to report: Inappropriate or harmful content Suspicious user behavior Any concerns involving potential child safety risks Reports are reviewed promptly, and necessary action is taken without delay. 6. Child Safety Contact We maintain a dedicated contact point for child safety concerns. Users, guardians, or authorities may contact us at: Email: support@shaadipariwar.com This channel is monitored for urgent safety-related issues and compliance matters. Prevention of Underage Use To maintain an adult-only environment: We clearly communicate that the platform is restricted to users aged 18+ We take action against accounts suspected of being operated by minors We may request age verification if suspicious activity is detected 8. Data Protection and Privacy We do not knowingly collect or store personal data of individuals under 18. If such data is identified, it will be promptly deleted. We follow applicable global data protection regulations, including: Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) 9. Content Guidelines for Adult Platform Even as an adult-focused platform, users must not: Post or share content involving minors in any context that is exploitative or inappropriate Misrepresent age or identity Engage in illegal or abusive activities 10. Compliance with Global Regulations We comply with applicable child safety and online protection laws across regions, including the United States, European Union, and other jurisdictions. 11. Policy Updates We may revise this policy periodically to reflect updates in legal or regulatory requirements. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of such changes. 12. Enforcement Actions Violations of this policy may result in: Immediate content removal Permanent suspension or termination of accounts Reporting to law enforcement or child protection authorities 13. User Responsibility By using our platform, users confirm that they are at least 18 years old and agree to comply with this Child Safety Policy and all applicable laws. Important Notice If you suspect child exploitation or abuse, please report it immediately using the contact form above or email us at support@shaadipariwar.com. Your report could help protect a child.
